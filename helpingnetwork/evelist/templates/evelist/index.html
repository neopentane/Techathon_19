Welcome to Helping Network

Non governmental bodies are doing a great job but what if their event goes unnoticed and they run out of funds ? Well, this happens quite frequently and especially for a newly established organisation, it becomes next to impossible to lay their foundation stone. There is just one solution to it - ACTIVE PUBLIC PARTICIPATION It's not uncommon to see half torn posters about the social events on the station wall. But this is definitely not the only way of creating awareness among the people. Time's changing rapidly. Newspapers are being replaced by electronic gadgets. Tech is the go-to tool to promote an event – or anything, really. It’s free and almost everyone uses it. After researching, we found that there are not many popular platforms where both organizer and volunteer share their interests and participate actively in the events happening. We have www.insider.in where people usually search for fun events. Now that's definitely not a place where social events can get a chance to stand. So, we need a fully dedicated platform just for the promotion of non profit events. Since a website can be accessed from anywhere and anytime, we chose to use this medium. Our main aim is to do a little for the betterment of the society through the knowledge that we possess. "Doing good is a collective responsibility and we all truly want to live in a better world" – Anshu Gupta, Founder Goonj
